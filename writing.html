How a project runs

Three stages, in order — each one has to hold before the next starts.

01 — DISCOVER

Scope & research

Understand the audience, the subject, and what "finished" actually needs to look like before any writing starts.

02 — DRAFT

Structure & write

Build the outline, then the full draft — organized so a reader can follow it without getting lost.

03 — REFINE

Review & deliver

Revise against feedback, check facts and consistency, and hand off a version that's ready to publish or submit.
